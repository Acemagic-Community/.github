# Welcome to Acemagic Community 🚀

We are a group of independent developers and enthusiasts dedicated to enhancing the user experience on Acemagic devices through open-source software and community-driven tools.

### ⚠️ Legal Disclaimer
**Acemagic Community** is an independent project. We are **not** affiliated, associated, authorized, endorsed by, or in any way officially connected with **Acemagic** or any of its subsidiaries or affiliates. All product names, logos, and brands are property of their respective owners.

### 🛠️ Our Mission
Our goal is to provide high-quality utilities and enhancements for the community, including:
- **Custom Tools**: Real-time on-screen displays for system stats and inputs and more.
- **System Utilities**: Optimization scripts and hardware-specific tools.
- **Community Support**: Documentation and guides created by users, for users.
